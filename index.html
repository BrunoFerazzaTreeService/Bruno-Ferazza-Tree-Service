About Us

At Bruno Ferazza Tree Services, we take pride in providing extraordinary tree care that reflects our promise of safety, honesty, and environmental responsibility. Our mission is to deliver unfailing solutions that protect your property and promote healthy, thriving trees. We aim to be Roseville’s most reliable tree care experts for homes and businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your main operational service area?
We are centrally based out of North Highlands, California, and cater to properties requiring premium tree styling and removal services throughout the region.
Are your tree removal services insured?
Yes, absolute safety and honest accountability are core pillars of Bruno Ferazza Tree Service. We carry comprehensive liability and structural safety insurance policies.
How does stump grinding benefit my lawn layout?
Stump removal clears deep underground wood, stopping root decay pests and opening valuable square footage for foundational hardscaping or lush replanting.
